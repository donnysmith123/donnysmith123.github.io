Before the Storm

Preparation is everything. Inlet Point Engineering provides proactive inspections of docks, lifts, and seawalls before major storms or hurricanes. We document your structure’s pre-storm condition, offering peace of mind and valuable documentation for your insurance provider.

After the Storm

We respond quickly. Within 24–72 hours of the storm’s passing, our team is on-site conducting structural inspections, capturing high-resolution drone footage, and preparing professional reports tailored for insurance claims. These include:

  • Detailed damage assessment
  • Drone photography and video
  • Code compliance review
  • Stamped engineer letters (if required)
