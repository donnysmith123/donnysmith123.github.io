About Us

Inlet Point Engineering is a locally rooted marine inspection and design firm proudly serving South Florida—specifically Martin and Palm Beach counties. With over 20 years of hands-on coastal experience, we specialize in dock and seawall inspections, engineering design, and permitting support.

We’re here for the community when it matters most. Whether before or after a storm, our detailed inspection reports help homeowners understand existing conditions and support insurance claims with confidence. We assess for structural damage from wind and flooding, and ensure code compliance to give property owners peace of mind.

Beyond inspections, we offer complete engineering solutions for new docks and seawalls. We work closely with clients to bring their vision to life, coordinating designs, navigating permits, and collaborating with trusted construction partners to ensure successful project delivery.

Our Services

Dock & Seawall Inspections

Pre- and post-hurricane evaluations to document structural integrity, assess damage, and ensure compliance for insurance and peace of mind.

Engineering & Design

Custom dock and seawall designs engineered for durability, compliance, and client vision. We also coordinate with construction teams for seamless execution.

Permitting Assistance

We guide clients through local, state, and federal permitting—ensuring smooth approvals. Our team stays involved from planning through construction handoff.
